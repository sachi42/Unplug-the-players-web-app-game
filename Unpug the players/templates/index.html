Let's understand the rules first:

  1. You will be shown images and names of 6 random players who are in FIFA 2020.
  2. Next you will be given 5 hints, one by one
  3. You can guess the player at any point of time by clicking on the image of that player.
  4. If you guess it wrong then you will be thrown back to the welcome page.
  5. If you guess it right, you will be given subsequent points.


    • 60 points- Answering on first hint
    • 50 points- Answering on second hint
    • 40 points- Answering on third hint
    • 20 points- Answering on fourth hint
    • 0 points - For not answering( LOL 😂 what else do you expect)
